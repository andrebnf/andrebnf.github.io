"Future Computer Scientist, sports enthusiast and coffee lover."

I've been doing programming and hardware maintenance since I was 14. The first particularity I realised about computing is the speed on which things change: databases, paradigms, architectures, hardwares, operating systems, the whole bunch. Well, are changes good in our life? Maybe no. But that's not the way I saw it: every day we come up with new approaches to old problems and that's great! This meant to me that I could work on an inovator environment that would be dynamic, where people could be keen to crete their own alternatves to general issues as well as to solve the company's problems. I wasn't wrong.
